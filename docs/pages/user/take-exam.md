# Take exam page

**Path:** `WEB-INF/jsp/user/take-exam.jsp`  
**Route:** `/user/exam`  
**Feature:** [take-exam flow](../../features/take-exam/flow.md)

One question at a time with progress (`Question X of Y`), dual timers (per-question share and overall exam), Prev/Next navigation, and submit on the last question. Selecting an option saves via AJAX.
